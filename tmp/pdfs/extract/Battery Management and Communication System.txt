High-Level Design
System partitioned into battery unit, control
electronics, and operator station to isolate the
high-energy pack from the USB host interface.

Battery Unit
- 10s1p pack, thermistors, fan, balancing, and primary
protection hardware.
- Custom BMS PCB

Testbenches
- Custom ELoad PCB to sink electrical energy and
dissipate as heat.
- Custom Cell Emulator to emulate individual cell voltages
Operator Station
- BMS Dashboard that displays battery data from the
BMS and controls the ELoad
- Battery Charger software to control the battery
charger for our battery pack.
